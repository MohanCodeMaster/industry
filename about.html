About KTS NEXUS Solutions

For over a decade, we have partnered with industrial developers to transition ambitious visions into statutory realities.

Our History & Mission

Founded in 2012, KTS NEXUS Solutions has established itself as Andhra Pradesh's premier industrial consultancy firm. We support companies through technical report writing (DPR, TEV, EMP) and government approvals (APPCB, Factory Licenses, FSSAI).

Our mission is to simplify complex government clearance procedures for businesses, fostering fast-track industrial growth while strictly adhering to ecological and regulatory standards.
